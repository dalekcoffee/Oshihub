## 🌐 What is OshiNetwork?

You may be wondering what this service is, who runs it? what's the point?, what do we do?
OshiNetwork is a passion project run by me, [Dalek](https://dalek.coffee)!
I run it both for personal use and also extending the benefits to communities I most closely relate with and wish to support.
OshiNetwork is NOT a business, and not for profit. **The goal is to cater to and support community!**
