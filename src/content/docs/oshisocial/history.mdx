## The Beginning: Baka.Social

<Aside type="note">
In December 2022, Baka.Social was created as a 🐘 **Mastodon** instance for the VR Creator community.
</Aside>

### Original Logo (2022)

<Frame>
  <img src={import.meta.env.BASE_URL + 'images/OriginalLogo.png'} alt="BakaSocial Logo" style={{ maxHeight: '150px', display: 'block', margin: '0 auto' }} />
</Frame>

The logo was a simplistic VRChat-themed design, which evolved into the "UwUBubble" design in 2023.

## Migration to Oshi.Social

<Aside type="note">
In 2024, we migrated from Baka.Social to Oshi.Social and switched from Mastodon to 🦈 **Sharkey** software.
</Aside>

### Why We Migrated
