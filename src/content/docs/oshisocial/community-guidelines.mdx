## 📋 Overview

OshiSocial focuses on VR creators but welcomes general posting that follows our rules and US law. We want everyone to feel comfortable here!

<Aside type="note">
These guidelines are designed to maintain a safe, welcoming, and creative environment for all community members. Please read them carefully before participating.
</Aside>

## 📏 Community Rules

### 1️⃣ Age Requirement

<Aside type="caution">
🔞 **You must be 18+ to join this server.**
</Aside>

### 2️⃣ Community Behavior

Be respectful to all members - we want everyone to feel comfortable here. Treat others as you would like to be treated.

### 3️⃣ NSFW Content Policy

<Aside type="note">
OshiSocial is **NOT** an NSFW-focused platform. We maintain strict guidelines on adult content.
</Aside>

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="✅ Encouraged">
    - Clean and wholesome content
    - Creative and artistic work
    - Community-friendly posts
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="⚠️ Requirements">
    - Sensitive content must be legal in the US
    - Must be properly content warned/blurred/marked sensitive
    - Cannot comprise the majority of your activity
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="🚫 Prohibited">
    - Content comprising most of a user's activity
    - IRL or self-posted explicit content
    - Self-harm, abuse against people/children/animals
    - Illegal content (under US law)
    - NSFW loli/shota (clothed VRC models are acceptable)
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

### 4️⃣ No Unsolicited Advances

Respect personal boundaries. Do not send unwanted romantic or sexual messages to other members.

### 5️⃣ Bot Usage

- Bots must be properly marked as bots
- Bot activity must be non-spammy
- Automated accounts must provide value to the community

### 6️⃣ Privacy & Safety

**No doxxing or sharing private information** - This includes real names, addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal information without consent.

### 7️⃣ Legal Compliance

No content that violates US law. This is non-negotiable and will result in immediate action.

### 8️⃣ Regional Eligibility

<Aside type="caution">
**OshiSocial does not accept registrations from individuals located in or residing in GDPR-covered territories**, including the European Union, European Economic Area, and United Kingdom.

By creating an account, you confirm you are not located in or a resident of any GDPR-covered territory.
</Aside>

<Aside type="note">
OshiSocial is a **free, non-commercial service** run out of the United States by a single individual with no revenue or funding. GDPR compliance for services offering registration to EU/EEA/UK residents requires appointing a formal EU representative - a legal obligation we simply cannot meet at this scale. Restricting registration to non-GDPR territories is the honest and responsible way to handle that limitation rather than ignoring it. If you're in one of these regions, we're sorry we can't accommodate you!
</Aside>

### 9️⃣ No Violence or Extremism

- No incitement to violence
- No promotion of violent ideologies
- No threatening behavior

### 🔟 Political Content

Political content requires appropriate content warnings. Keep discussions respectful and constructive.

### 1️⃣1️⃣ No Spam

No spam accounts, excessive self-promotion, or repetitive content that degrades the user experience.
