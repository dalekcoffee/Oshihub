## Reporting & Moderation

OshiSocial moderates content from both local users and remote instances across the fediverse. We follow our [Community Guidelines](/oshisocial/community-guidelines) for all moderation decisions.

<Aside type="note">
Please report posts directly within the application for the fastest response.
</Aside>

### Reporting Guidelines

When reporting content, please keep in mind:

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Reporting Accounts" icon="information">
    When reporting accounts, include **at least a few posts that show rule-breaking behavior**, when applicable.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Provide Context" icon="list-format">
    Include additional context in your report comments. This is **especially important when the content is in a language nobody on the moderation team speaks**.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## DMCA Requests

For DMCA takedown requests, please send your request to:
**Email:** help@oshi.social

<Aside type="caution">
All DMCA requests must follow proper reporting procedure as per **17 U.S.C. 512(c)(3)**
</Aside>

<Aside type="note">
We process all valid DMCA requests in accordance with United States copyright law and will respond as required by law.
</Aside>
