## Eligibility Requirements

## How to Apply

<Aside type="note">
In order to verify your identity and prevent creator impersonation.
</Aside>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Your Handle">
    Please add your desired handle to your primary platform and place it somewhere obvious/easy to find such as your channel bio or a twitch panel. You can remove this after your account is approved!

    **(Example: @username@oshi.social)**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Visit OshiSocial">
    Visit [https://oshi.social/](https://oshi.social/) and click on "Sign up at this instance"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit Application">
    Please submit this info in the "Reason" for joining along with any message you want to pass to staff.

    **(Example: Hey it's me! the handle is in my bio @ https://twitch.tv/yourname)**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for Approval">
    Please allow up to 48 hours for approval. We will try to get to these ASAP, but we might not be immediately available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Aside type="note">
Accounts that fail to submit the requested info under the reason for joining will be automatically rejected with or without notice.
If you submitted the above info and have not heard back after 48hrs, feel free to contact us via email: help@oshi.social
</Aside>
