## 💌 Contributions 💌

This is currently a passion project 100% funded by [@Dalekcoffee@oshi.social](https://oshi.social/@Dalekcoffee).

**Why no donations?**
Donations are not accepted at this time due to the tax complications that would arise from either:
- Mixing donations with personal assets
- Establishing a separate legal entity for the platform

The current single-source funding model ensures simplicity and full control over the platform's direction.

## 🏷️ Advertising 🏷️

**Our Commitment:**
We will never display advertisements that violate our Community Guidelines.

**Our Goal:**
Should we ever implement advertising, it will be used for fun and informational purposes that align with our community culture and values.

**Future Advertising Vision**

Any future advertising implementation would prioritize:
- Community relevance
- Entertainment value
- Educational content
- Strict adherence to our guidelines
