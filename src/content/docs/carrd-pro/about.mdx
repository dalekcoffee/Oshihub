## Why Carrd Pro?

You can already use Carrd for free without joining the Oshi tenant, but the free tier has some limitations.
Joining us on the Oshi tenant gives you access to **Pro Standard features** including premium templates and custom domains!

### Additional Pro Features

<Accordion title="View all Pro Standard features">
  - **Unlimited Elements**: No restrictions on elements per site
  - **High Quality Images**: Support for files up to 16MB (images/GIFs) and 64MB (videos)
  - **Custom Icons & Share Images**: Set custom site icons and social media share images
  - **Google Analytics**: Integrate analytics to track your site's performance
  - **Forms**: Add contact, signup, and other form types to collect information
  - **Embeds & Widgets**: Integrate third-party services seamlessly
  - **Priority Support**: Get help when you need it
</Accordion>
